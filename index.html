    What we do

    Air Quality Systems East Africa is a leading provider of comprehensive air quality monitoring solutions in the East African region. Our mission is to ensure cleaner air and healthier lives through cutting-edge technology and reliable monitoring systems.
    Collaborative Approach and Expertise: Collaboration with Passam AG enhances our capabilities for a comprehensive approach to air quality management.Integration of manufacturing and analysis processes for expertise and reliability in operations.

    Here are some the services we offer in Summary:

    • Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) Monitoring.
    • Particulate Matter Monitoring (PM2.5 & PM10)-.
    • Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) Monitoring.
    • Sulphur Dioxide (SO2) Monitoring.
    • Carbon Monoxide (CO) Monitoring.
    • Ozone (O3) Monitoring.
    • BTEX (Benzene, Toluene, Ethylbenzene, Xylene) Monitoring.

    • Formaldehyde (CH2O) Monitoring.

    Explore our range of products

    PASSAM AG NH3

    The PASSAM AG Sampler NH3 is a passive device used to measure ammonia (NH3) in the air. It captures ammonia on a chemically treated filter for later analysis.

    PASSAM AG Sampler BTEX

    The PASSAM AG Sampler BTEX is a passive device used to measure benzene, toluene, ethylbenzene, and xylenes (BTEX) in the air. It absorbs these compounds on a sorbent material for later analysis.

    PASSAM AG Sampler CO

    The PASSAM AG Sampler CO is a passive device used to measure carbon monoxide (CO) in the air. It captures CO on a chemically treated absorbent for later analysis.
