About Our Company

Air Quality Systems East Africa is a leading provider of comprehensive air quality monitoring solutions in the East African region. Our mission is to ensure cleaner air and healthier lives through cutting-edge technology and reliable monitoring systems.

We offer a diverse range of products tailored to meet the needs of both outdoor and indoor air quality assessment. Our product line includes:

  • Passive Samplers: Designed for accurate detection and measurement of all gaseous pollutants. These samplers provide an efficient and cost-effective way to monitor air quality over extended periods.
  • Active Monitors: We provide a broad spectrum of active monitoring devices, from low-cost options to high-end models capable of measuring particulate matter (PM) across various size ranges (PM1, PM2.5, PM10). These monitors deliver real-time data critical for understanding and managing air pollution.
  • Indoor Air Quality Monitors: Our advanced indoor air quality monitors are designed to track pollutants and ensure safe and healthy environments within homes, offices, and industrial settings.

With our state-of-the-art equipment and dedicated support, we empower communities, industries, and governments to take proactive steps in improving air quality and safeguarding public health. Whether you are conducting large-scale environmental assessments or focused on improving the air quality of a single building, Air Quality Systems East Africa has the expertise and solutions to meet your needs.
